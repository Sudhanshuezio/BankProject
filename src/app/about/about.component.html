About us

Sharmaji Bank is India's largest private sector bank with total consolidated assets of Rs. 13846.83 billion (US$ 200.5 billion) at March 31, 2018 and profit after tax of Rs. 67.77 billion (US$ 1.0 billion) for the year ended March 31, 2018. Sharmaji Bank currently has a network of 6,341 Branches and 20,637 ATMs across India.

History

Sharmaji Bank was originally promoted in 1996 by Sharmaji Limited, an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
